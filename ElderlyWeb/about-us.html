About Us

The Petra Elderly Centre, a comprehensive elderly care institution

Experience dignified living with our need-based accommodations, exceptional healthcare services, and a vibrant community that fosters social connections. We prioritize your comfort and well-being, ensuring that you thrive in an environment tailored to your individual needs. Join us in a nurturing space where care meets community, providing not just a place to stay, but a place to truly belong  

Space for senior citizens to stay physically active and engaged by participating in various  activities and programs. A platform for senior citizens to engage and contribute to socioeconomic issues, intergenerational conversations, and to harness elders' knowledge, experiences, and wisdom.
